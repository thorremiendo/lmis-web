MULAT-BLISTT RESEARCH PROGRAM

Monitored by DOST-PCIEERD and funded by DOST.

The research program was designed to produce up-to-date, high resolution information on Rainfall-Induced Landslides (RIL) in order to lower and prevent risks and losses in lives, livelihoods, health, community assets, and environmental quality. It was also intended to provide information for policies for early warning, evacuation, and development planning. Its goals are to support disaster mitigation and to strengthen disaster resilience.

Project 1:

Modelling Susceptibility and Generating Rainfall-Threshold for Rainfall-Induced Landslide

Project 2:

CALAMI-T - Cordillera Administrative LAndslide Monitoring Integrated Toolbox

The MULAT-BLISTT research program is a two-part collaboration of the the (UP-Baguio) University of the Philippines Baguio-(MGB-CAR) Mines and Geosciences Bureau-CAR, (UC) University of the Cordilleras-(UB) University of Baguio, (DOST-CAR) Department of Science and Technology - Cordillera Administrative Region, and (DOST-PCIEERD) Department of Science and Technology Philippine Council for Industry, Energy, Emerging Technology Research & Development.
