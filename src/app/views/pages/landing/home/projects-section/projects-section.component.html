Project 1

Modelling Susceptibility and Generating Rainfall-Threshold for Rainfall-Induced Landslide

One of the most exposed areas to rainfall-induced landslides is the BLISTT area of the Cordillera Administrative Region, which experienced nine out of ten of the country's greatest 24-hour rainfall totals during tropical cyclones.

The MULAT-BLISTT, which stands for Modelling and Understanding Landslide Events in BLISTT (Baguio, La Trinidad, Itogon, Sablan, Tuba, Tublay), is a research program funded by the DOST-PCIEERD. The program's objective is to increase disaster resilience in accordance with R.A. 10121, the "Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act of 2010," and the UN 2030 Sustainable Development Goal.

Catalogue of rainfall, extreme rainfall, and typhoon occurrence

The catalogue summarizes rainfall, extreme rainfall events, and typhoon occurrences over the past two decades.

Catalogue and Database of Landslide reports

The Catalogue and Database compiles comprehensive landslide reports for analysis and mitigation.

Rainfall Threshold for Landslides

The rainfall threshold for a landslide can vary depending on factors like soil type and terrain.

Landslide Susceptibility, Vulnerability and Risk Maps

Assess and mitigate the potential for landslides in a given area.

Publication of Journals

Journals are published to disseminate the latest research findings and promote academic discourse.

Project 2

CALaMI-T - Cordillera Administrative Landslide Monitoring Integrated Toolbox

Project CALaMI-T is a landslide mitigation research and development for BLISTT. It aims to develop a landslide movement model toolbox that can predict landslides and provide information on the impact of rain-induced landslides on communities, infrastructure, and the environment.

It is the Project 2 under the Program MULAT BLISTT: Modelling and Understanding LAndslide evenTs in Baguio-La Trinidad-Itogon-Sablan-Tuba-Tublay.

Soil Moisture Threshold

Determine soil moisture threshold values and other parameters for landslide.

Landslide Movement Model Toolbox Software

Develop or adopt a suitable landslide movement model toolbox software.

Landsilde Management Information System

Develop and testing of a landslde management information system.

Mobile Application for Landslide

Develop a mobile application for landslide warning and awareness, reporting and routing to evacuation areas or safe spots.

Early Warning System

Develop a Web and SMS based early warning system with DRRMO Operation Center Dashboard.
