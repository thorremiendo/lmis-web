PROJECT OVERVIEW

Project CALaMI-T is a landslide mitigation researc and development for BLISTT. It aims to develop a landslide movement model toolbox that can predict landslides and provide information on the impact of rain-induced landslides on communities, infrastructure, and the environment. It is the Project 2 under the Program MULAT BLISTT: Modelling and Understanding LAndslide evenTs in Baguio-La Trinidad-Itogon-Sablan-Tuba-Tublay.
